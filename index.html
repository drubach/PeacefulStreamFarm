Beef

We bring newly weaned cattle to our farm and they are raised on hay, clover, natural salt and minerals. That's it. No hormones or other factory farm tricks to help them mature faster. They roam the pastures at will. Processed humanely and available year-round.

Packaged - $4.50 per lb

Pork

We purchase our weaned piglets locally and then raise them entirely on organic grains, fruits and vegetables. Just like our beef, no factory farm methods to speed up weight gain are used. They root and explore their yard freely. Processed humanely and available year-round.
